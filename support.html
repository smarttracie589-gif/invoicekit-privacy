Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an invoice?

Tap the "+" button on the invoice list screen. Fill in your client details, add line items, and tap "Save". Your invoice is ready to share as a PDF.

How do I send an invoice?

Open any invoice and tap the share icon. You can send via email, WhatsApp, AirDrop, or any other app on your device.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

How many invoices can I create for free?

You can create up to 3 invoices per month on the free plan. Upgrade to InvoiceKit Pro for unlimited invoices.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the App Store on your iPhone, tap your profile photo, then go to Subscriptions. Find InvoiceKit and tap Cancel Subscription.

My data is gone after reinstalling. What happened?

InvoiceKit stores all data locally on your device. Data is not backed up to the cloud, so reinstalling the app will remove local invoice data. We recommend using the share feature to keep PDF copies of important invoices.
